Geospatial Data Model Manual
======================
The purpose of this Geospatial Data Model Manual is to provide guidance preparing digital survey databases to be delievered to RATES partners.  This is not intended to specify the process used, but to present standards and requirements of outpusts and deliverables.

The Survey Manual will provide specific details of data collection methods to meet RATES partner and industry standard deliverables.
